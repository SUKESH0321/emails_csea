Infinitum 2026 - Code of Conduct

Greetings,
We kindly ask all participants to carefully read and adhere to the following Code of Conduct for Infinitum 2026. Any form of misconduct will not be tolerated. The safety and well-being of all attendees are our priority.

Behavior
All participants are expected to conduct themselves in a respectful and courteous manner. Inappropriate, aggressive, or offensive behavior toward other participants, event convenors, faculty, stall representatives, or Infinitum volunteers will not be tolerated. Unnecessary roaming around the campus is discouraged. The event schedule and campus map will be provided via email. Any form of cyber attacks on payment systems or databases will be severely punished and reported.
Prohibited Items
The following items are strictly prohibited at the event: firearms, fireworks, weapons, drugs, alcohol, and any illegal or hazardous materials. Any violation of this rule will result in immediate expulsion from the event.
Food Policy
There will be 10+ food stalls available throughout the event for participants. Stalls accept cash and UPI payments. Food is allowed only near the food stalls; participants are prohibited from taking food into event venues.
Use of Technology
Participants are expected to use the technology and equipment provided at the event responsibly. Any misuse of technology, such as unauthorized access or tampering, will result in immediate disqualification.
Intellectual Property
All projects and presentations must be original and the intellectual property of the participants. Violation of intellectual property rights will result in immediate disqualification.
Rights to Photography and Videography
Photography and videography are permitted at the event, but participants must obtain explicit consent from any individuals being photographed or recorded.
Disqualification
Violating the rules and regulations may result in immediate disqualification. The decision of the event organizers is final. The respective college authorities of the disqualified participant will be notified.
Belongings
Participants are responsible for their belongings throughout the event. The organizers and the institution are not responsible for any loss or damage to personal belongings.
Damage to Property
Any damage to college or hostel property will result in the participant being fined. Please be mindful to avoid causing any damage to property during the event.
Best Wishes,
Infinitum Team,
