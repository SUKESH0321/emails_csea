Greetings,
We are thrilled to have you join KRIYA 2025! To ensure a seamless and enjoyable experience for all participants, we’ve outlined a set of rules and guidelines. Please take a moment to review these details before the event.

Timings ID Cards
The Kriya Registration Kiosks will be open from 8 AM onwards on all three days. Here you can collect your Kriya Kits and register for events and workshops on-site. Participants must carry a valid college ID card at all times. This is required for both registration and event entry. Without a valid ID, entry may be denied. The college ID is essential for gaining access to the college premises.
Inauguration Ceremony Dress Code
The Inauguration Ceremony of Kriya 2025 will take place on 14th March 2025 from 9:00 AM to 9:30 AM at the D-Block Conference Hall. We request all participants to kindly be seated 10 minutes before the ceremony begins. In terms of dress code, for men, formal attire is expected, and for women, a kurta with dupatta is required. Please ensure you adhere to this dress code to maintain a professional atmosphere during the event.For men, formal attire is required, while for women, a kurta with dupatta is expected.
Kriya Kit Event Preparation
Your Kriya 2025 kit bag and ID wrist tag will be issued at the Registration Kiosk. Please make sure to collect your kit and wear the wrist tag at all times. You won’t be allowed entry to events without it. Ensure you have all the required materials for your events and workshops. The necessary items will be listed in the event/workshop descriptions on the website. While we’ll provide essential technology and equipment, we recommend bringing any additional materials you might need.

Pro Show Certificates
The pro show is scheduled for 25th March 2023 at 5:30 PM. You will be required to show your KRIYA Wrist tags to enter. Participants who have paid the general registration fee are allowed to enter. On-Duty for PSG Tech Students On-Duty for other Students
PSG Tech students attending events will be provided On-Duty (OD). Attendance will be recorded for each event, and OD will be granted day-wise, specifying whether it's for the forenoon or afternoon. PSG College of Technology does not have authority to provide on-duty certificates for students from other colleges. Students from other colleges may use their Certificate of Participation or payment receipt emails to request on-duty status from their respective institutions.
